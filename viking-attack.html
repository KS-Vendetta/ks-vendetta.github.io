🛡️ Viking Attack

Viking Attack is a 20-wave alliance PvE event where Vikings attack alliance members and the Alliance Headquarters (HQ). Success depends on teamwork, proper reinforcements, and following the battle strategy.

Unlike many events, you earn most of your points by reinforcing other members, not by defending your own city.

🎁 Rewards

  • 🏆 Personal Ranking Rewards
  • 🎁 Alliance Milestone Rewards
  • 💎 Resources and valuable items
  • 📈 Event Points based on your contribution

The better the alliance performs, the better the rewards for everyone!

⚔️ How the Event Works

  • The event consists of 20 attack waves.
  • Most waves target alliance members.
  • Some waves attack online players only.
  • Waves 10 & 20 attack the Alliance Headquarters.

🤝 Reinforcement Strategy

During most waves, reinforce other alliance members to earn points.

  • ✅ Send Infantry only when reinforcing members.
  • 🚫 Do NOT send Archers when reinforcing players.
  • Archers do not earn reinforcement points.

🏰 HQ Defense (Rounds 10 & 20)

  • Reinforce the Alliance HQ immediately.
  • Use your best heroes.
  • Send 100% Infantry & Calvary (or follow leadership instructions).
  • Save a troop formation of approximately 43K highest-tier troops before the event.

📋 Event Guidelines

  • Put your strongest hero in the Guard Station.
  • Empty your city and reinforce alliance members.
  • If offline, share your city coordinates in Alliance Chat.
  • Do NOT use a Shield.
  • Heal troops after Wave 9 or after the event.
  • Form small reinforcement groups for better coordination.

🔥 Important Battle Tips

If the city you're reinforcing receives two successful Viking attacks, recall your troops immediately and reinforce another alliance member whose city is still standing.

⚠️ Remember

  • Reinforce teammates quickly.
  • Follow R5/R4 instructions.
  • Keep your city empty whenever possible.
  • Be ready for HQ battles during Rounds 10 & 20.
  • Teamwork earns the highest rewards.

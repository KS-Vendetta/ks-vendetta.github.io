# ⚔️ Swordland Showdown

Fight. Capture. Defend. Dominate.

Swordland Showdown is a large-scale PvP battlefield where alliances compete to capture objectives, defeat enemies, and earn valuable personal and alliance rewards. Teamwork and smart strategy matter far more than individual power.

---

# 📋 Pre-Event Checklist

Before Swordland Showdown begins, please complete your deployment:

✅ Select **ONE Legion** (**Legion 1** or **Legion 2**) and tap **Vote** under **Deployment**.

🚫 For the **other Legion**, select **Abstention** under **Deployment**.

⚠️ **Only register if you're confident you'll be available for the event.** Matchmaking is based on the number of registered players, so registering but not participating can put the entire team at a disadvantage.

🎯 This helps leadership balance both teams and organize the event efficiently.

💪 Thank you, and good luck on the battlefield!

---

# 🎯 Main Goal

Your **highest priority** is reaching **180,000 Personal Points**.

This unlocks the **highest personal reward tier**, regardless of whether your alliance wins or loses.

💡 As an F2P player, it's often better to be a top performer on the losing side than a low scorer on the winning side.

🚫 Don't sit idle or hide—keep earning points throughout the match.

---

# 🏰 Objectives

Capture and defend strategic buildings to earn points for yourself and your alliance.

### Main Objectives

- 🏰 Sanctums
- ⚔️ Sword Shrine
- 🐎 Royal Stables
- 🔔 Bell Tower

Holding objectives provides continuous points and valuable battlefield advantages.

---

# 🛡️ Defensive Role (Castle Defender)

If you're assigned to defend a captured objective:

## Recommended Heroes

- Jabel + Gordon

### Why?

- Excellent Infantry Defense
- High Infantry Health
- Outstanding survivability for holding buildings

📍 **Important:** Keep your city **1–2 screens away** from the objective you're defending.

Never teleport directly beside the building, as stronger players may attack your city instead of the objective.

---

# ⚔️ Offensive Role

Avoid fighting stronger players head-on.

Instead:

- Attack isolated enemies.
- Capture lightly defended objectives.
- Support alliance pushes.
- Reinforce important rallies.

## Recommended Heroes

- Chenko + Quinn
- Chenko + Saul

### Why?

Chenko provides excellent **Lethality** bonuses while Quinn and Saul add strong offensive damage.

---

# 🤝 Rally Joiners

When joining major alliance rallies (especially Sword Shrine):

## Recommended Lead Hero

✅ Chenko

Only your **first hero's top-right expedition skill** applies inside rallies.

Chenko's **Lethality Buff** greatly increases the rally's overall damage.

---

# ⚡ F2P Survival Tricks

## ❤️ Free Hospital Trick

There are **no permanent troop deaths** during Swordland Showdown.

If your army is defeated and your hospital is full:

- Leave the battlefield.
- Your entire army instantly heals.
- Re-enter after the **12-minute cooldown**.

⚠️ Avoid doing this during the final **15 minutes** of the event.

---

## 🏕️ Encampment Trick

If a much stronger enemy is marching toward your city:

- Send all troops to an Encampment.
- Leave your city empty.

Your city may burn and teleport back safely while your troops remain untouched.

---

# ✅ Before the Battle

✔ Clear your Home Kingdom Hospital.

Even **one wounded troop** prevents you from entering Swordland Showdown.

✔ Save your best troop formations.

✔ Carry enough teleports.

✔ Repair and prepare all marches.

✔ Be online before matchmaking begins.

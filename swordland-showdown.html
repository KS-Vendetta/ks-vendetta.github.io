⚔️ Swordland Showdown

Fight. Capture. Defend. Dominate.

Swordland Showdown is a large-scale PvP battlefield where alliances compete to capture objectives, defeat enemies, and earn valuable personal and alliance rewards. Teamwork and smart strategy matter far more than individual power.

📋 Pre-Event Checklist

  • Select ONE Legion (Legion 1 or Legion 2) and tap Vote under Deployment.
  • For the other Legion, choose Abstention.
  • Only register if you can participate for the full event.
  • Leadership uses registrations to balance both teams.
🎯 Register responsibly—it helps create fair matchmaking and stronger teams.

⭐ Quick Tips

  • 🎯 Reach 180,000 Personal Points.
  • 🛡️ Defend assigned objectives.
  • ⚔️ Attack isolated enemies, not whales.
  • 🤝 Join rallies using Chenko.
  • ❤️ Use the Free Hospital Trick.
  • 🏕️ Use the Encampment Trick.
  • 📍 Stay with your alliance and follow R5/R4 instructions.

🎯 Main Goal

Your highest priority is reaching 180,000 Personal Points, which unlocks the highest personal reward tier regardless of whether your alliance wins or loses.

For many F2P players, earning maximum personal rewards is more valuable than chasing unnecessary fights.

🏰 Objectives

Capture and defend strategic buildings.

  • 🏰 Sanctums
  • ⚔️ Sword Shrine
  • 🐎 Royal Stables
  • 🔔 Bell Tower

Holding objectives continuously earns points for both you and your alliance.

🛡️ Defensive Role

Recommended Heroes

  • Jabel + Gordon

Why?

  • Excellent Infantry Defense
  • High Infantry Health
  • Great survivability while holding buildings

Keep your city 1–2 screens away from the building you're defending. Never teleport directly beside the objective.

⚔️ Offensive Role

  • Attack isolated enemies.
  • Capture lightly defended objectives.
  • Support alliance pushes.
  • Reinforce rallies.

Recommended Heroes

  • Chenko + Quinn
  • Chenko + Saul

Chenko provides powerful Lethality buffs while Quinn and Saul add strong offensive damage.

🤝 Rally Joiners

Use Chenko as your lead hero whenever possible.

Only the top-right Expedition Skill of your first hero applies in rallies, making Chenko's Lethality buff extremely valuable.

⚡ F2P Survival Tricks

❤️ Free Hospital Trick

  • No permanent troop deaths occur during Swordland.
  • If your hospital fills up, leave the battlefield.
  • Your troops heal instantly.
  • Re-enter after the 12-minute cooldown.
  • Avoid doing this during the final 15 minutes.

🏕️ Encampment Trick

  • Send all troops to an Encampment.
  • Leave your city empty.
  • Your city may burn, but your troops remain safe.

✅ Before the Battle

  • Clear your Home Kingdom Hospital.
  • Save your best troop formations.
  • Carry enough teleports.
  • Prepare all marches.
  • Be online before matchmaking begins.

🏰 Strategic Buildings

🐎 Royal Stables

Reduces Teleport Cooldown, allowing faster movement across the battlefield.

🔔 Bell Tower

Increases Capture Speed, helping your alliance secure objectives faster.

📋 Battle Guidelines

  • Stay with your alliance.
  • Follow R5/R4 instructions.
  • Teleport only when instructed.
  • Reinforce allies whenever requested.
  • Defend assigned objectives.
  • Join rallies immediately.
  • Avoid unnecessary solo fights.

🏆 Victory Strategy

Swordland Showdown isn't about getting the most kills.

  • 🎯 Reach 180,000 Personal Points
  • 🏰 Capture Objectives
  • 🤝 Support Your Alliance
  • ⚔️ Win as a Team
💙 Strong players win battles. Strong alliances win wars.

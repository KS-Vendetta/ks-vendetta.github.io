🏆 Alliance Championship

Plan. Register. Win.

Alliance Championship is a strategic PvP event where proper registration is just as important as troop strength. Following the alliance strategy gives us the best chance of victory.

📋 Registration

  • ✅ Register in the Middle Lane ONLY.
  • 🚫 Do NOT register in the Left or Right Lane unless instructed by Leadership.
  • 💡 Leadership will reorganize players after registration to create the strongest possible lineup.
Why Middle Lane?

Matchmaking is based on the Top 20 combatants in each lane. Registering everyone in the Middle allows Leadership to redistribute players strategically across all three lanes.

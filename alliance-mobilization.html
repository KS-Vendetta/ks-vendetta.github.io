📋 Alliance Mobilization

Complete missions. Earn points. Unlock rewards.

Alliance Mobilization is a team event where members complete missions to earn points for themselves and the alliance. The more points earned, the better the milestone rewards for everyone.

📋 Before the Event

  • ✅ Save Construction, Research and Training Speedups.
  • ✅ Delay completing Construction and Research until the event begins.
  • ✅ Keep enough resources available to complete missions.
  • ✅ Refresh low-value missions and prioritize high-point tasks.

⭐ Quick Tips

  • 🎯 Complete high-value missions.
  • 🔄 Refresh poor-value missions.
  • ⏳ Save speedups for the event.
  • 🏗️ Time your research and construction.
  • 🏆 Reach every personal milestone.
  • 🤝 Every member should contribute.

🎁 Rewards

Personal Rewards

  • ⏱️ Speedups
  • 💎 Gems
  • 🪵 Resources
  • 🧱 Building Materials
  • 🦸 Hero Upgrade Materials
  • 📦 Personal Milestone Chests
  • 🏅 Event Ranking Rewards

Alliance Rewards

  • 📦 Alliance Milestone Chests
  • ⏱️ Speedups
  • 💎 Gems
  • 🪵 Resources
  • 🦸 Hero Upgrade Materials
  • Additional event rewards

The more members contribute, the better the rewards for everyone.

📋 Best Mission Types

Prioritize missions that provide the highest points for the least effort.

  • 🏗️ Construction
  • 🔬 Research
  • ⚔️ Troop Training
  • 🌲 Resource Gathering
  • 🐻 Beast Hunting

⚠️ Refresh missions that require excessive resources for very few points.

🚫 Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Completing Construction or Research before the event starts.
  • ❌ Accepting low-point missions.
  • ❌ Letting missions expire.
  • ❌ Using speedups on poor-value tasks.
  • ❌ Ignoring easy gathering missions.

💙 VEN Tips

  • 💡 Save Construction, Research and Training speedups specifically for Alliance Mobilization whenever possible.
  • 💡 If you're unsure whether a mission is worth completing, ask an R4 before spending resources or speedups.
  • 💡 If you're pushing for leaderboard rankings, consider saving your speedups for the final hours.
  • 💡 Even if you can't reach the top rankings, completing your personal milestones helps the entire alliance unlock better rewards.
Every point matters.

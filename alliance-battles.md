# ⚔️ Alliance Battles

Alliance Battles include **Sanctuaries, Fortresses, and Outposts**. These are large-scale PvP events where teamwork, coordination, and strategy are more important than individual power.

---

## 🏰 Why Are Alliance Battles Important?

Winning these battles helps both **you** and the **alliance** by providing:

- 🏆 Valuable personal and alliance rewards.
- 💎 Alliance Honor and event rewards.
- 📈 Strategic buffs and map control.
- 👑 Increased alliance influence across the kingdom.
- 💀 PvP kills and battle experience.
- 🤝 Better teamwork and rally coordination.

**Remember:** Every member's participation contributes to the alliance's success!

---

# ⚔️ When Attacking

Use your **best 3 heroes** and focus on **offensive heroes**.

## 🤝 Rally Joiners
Join rallies using **ONE** of the following heroes as your **left hero**:

- Chenko *(Lethality %)*
- Yeonwoo *(Lethality %)*
- Amane *(Attack %)*
- Amadeus *(Lethality %)*

### Recommended Troop Formation

| Infantry | Cavalry | Archer |
|:--------:|:-------:|:------:|
| **50%** | **20%** | **30%** |

If you don't have any of the above heroes, **join without heroes**.

---

# 🛡️ Defending / Garrison

When reinforcing a building, use one of these heroes as your **left hero**:

- Howard *(Defense)*
- Gordon *(Health)*
- Saul *(Defense & Health)*

### Recommended Troop Formation

| Infantry | Cavalry | Archer |
|:--------:|:-------:|:------:|
| **70%** | **30%** | **0%** |

---

# ❤️ Healing Troops

Healing efficiently keeps you battle-ready.

- Heal troops **immediately** after battles.
- Heal in **small batches (under 500 troops)**.
- Tap **Alliance Help** every time to reduce healing time.
- Repeat until all troops are fully healed.

---

# ⚠️ Battle Guidelines

📍 Be prepared to **teleport** near the battle location when instructed by the **R5** or **R4 Leadership Team**.

✅ Once instructed, move your city to the **alliance territory** before the battle begins.

✅ Leave the **front-line teleport spots** closest to the objective for the **designated rally leaders**. Other members should teleport just behind them.

⚔️ Rally leaders will be assigned by the **R5/R4 Leadership Team** before each battle. If you're selected, please be ready to lead rallies.

🤝 All other members should focus on **joining rallies quickly** and reinforcing when instructed.

✅ Use **Level 7 troops or higher** whenever possible.

✅ Always use the **recommended heroes** when joining rallies or defending.

⚠️ Rally Hosts and Garrison Leaders may remove players using incorrect heroes. Simply update your march and rejoin.

🚫 Do **not** send solo attacks unless instructed by leadership.

🎯 Follow all instructions from the **R5/R4 Leadership Team** during the battle. Coordination wins battles!

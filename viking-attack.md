# 🛡️ Viking Attack

Viking Attack is a **20-wave alliance PvE event** where Vikings attack alliance members and the Alliance Headquarters (HQ). Success depends on teamwork, proper reinforcements, and following the battle strategy.

Unlike many events, **you earn most of your points by reinforcing other members**, not by defending your own city.

---

## 🎁 Rewards

Participating in Viking Attack rewards players with:

- 🏆 Personal Ranking Rewards
- 🎁 Alliance Milestone Rewards
- 💎 Resources and valuable items
- 📈 Event Points based on your contribution

The better the alliance performs, the better the rewards for everyone!

---

# ⚔️ How the Event Works

The event consists of **20 attack waves**.

- Most waves target alliance members.
- Some waves only attack **online players**.
- Waves **10 & 20** attack the **Alliance Headquarters (HQ)**.

Your goal is to defend each other, maximize points, and prevent unnecessary troop losses.

---

# 🌊 Wave Structure

| Wave | Target |
|------|--------|
| 1 – 6 | All Members |
| 7 | Online Members Only |
| 8 – 9 | All Members |
| 10 | Alliance HQ |
| 11 – 13 | All Members |
| 14 | Online Members Only |
| 15 – 16 | All Members |
| 17 | Online Members Only |
| 18 – 19 | All Members |
| 20 | Alliance HQ |

---

# 🤝 Reinforcement Strategy

During most waves, reinforce **other alliance members** to earn points.

### Reinforcement Rules

✅ Send **Infantry only** when reinforcing members.

🚫 **DO NOT send Archers** when reinforcing players.
Archers **do not earn reinforcement points**.

---

# 🏰 HQ Defense (Rounds 10 & 20)

When the HQ is attacked:

- Reinforce the Alliance HQ immediately.
- Use your **best heroes**.
- Send **100% Archers** (or follow the current leadership strategy if instructed otherwise).

For maximum HQ contribution:

💡 Save a troop formation of approximately **43K highest-tier troops** before the event begins.

---

# 📋 Event Guidelines

✅ Put your **strongest hero** in the **Guard Station**.

✅ **Empty your city!**
Send your troops to reinforce alliance members instead of leaving them at home.

📍 If you're unable to participate, send your reinforcements before going offline and **share your city coordinates in Alliance Chat** so members can reinforce your city.

🚫 **Do NOT use a Shield.**
Shielding prevents Vikings from attacking your city and reduces overall alliance points.

❤️ Do **not heal troops during battle** if it recalls your troops from reinforcements.
Instead, heal after **Wave 9** before the HQ battle or after the event if possible.

🤝 Form small support groups (pairs or groups of six) to reinforce one another consistently throughout the event.

---

# 🔥 Important Battle Tips

If the city you're reinforcing receives **two successful Viking attacks**, recall your troops immediately and reinforce another alliance member whose city is still standing.

This helps maximize points while avoiding unnecessary troop losses.

---

# ⚠️ Remember

- Reinforce teammates quickly.
- Follow R5/R4 instructions.
- Keep your city empty whenever possible.
- Be ready for HQ battles on **Rounds 10 & 20**.
- Teamwork earns the highest rewards!
